About the Program

IIT Hyderabad and Deakin University are pleased to announce the opening of registrations for the jointly supervised Doctorate program. Scholars will benefit from dual supervision, international exposure, and world-class research facilities.

This unique program offers an exceptional opportunity for PhD candidates to work with leading academics and researchers from both institutions, gaining a truly global perspective on their research.

Program Highlights

Global Degree

Receive a doctoral degree from both Deakin University and IIT Hyderabad.

Dual Supervision

Access world-class research facilities at both institutions with guidance from experts at both universities.

International Exposure

Gain expertise through cross-continental research and global academic networks.

Generous Scholarship

Equivalent to the Australian RTP rates ($A35,550 per annum 2025 rate) indexed annually and granted pro rata.

Travel Allowance

Return economy airfare to Australia and visa application fee (approx. A$4,000).

Liberal Arts Welcome

Registration from Liberal Arts students are welcome across various disciplines.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completion of a degree at level 8 or 9 of the Australian Qualifications Framework, with a minimum overall grade equivalent to a Deakin grade of 70%.
  • The qualifying degree must be in the relevant area and include a research component, equivalent to 25% of the final year's full-time study.
  • The research component must include a dissertation or equivalent assessed outputs with a minimum achievement grade equivalent to a Deakin grade of 70%.
  • Language proficiency: IELTS (Academic), TOEFL iBT, or PTE (Academic) scores are mandatory.
Important Note:

TOEFL tests taken between 26 July 2023 and 4 May 2024 are not valid for Australian visa purposes.

Open to PhD scholars enrolled in July 2024 and Jan 2025. Applicants must be within the first 6 months of their PhD enrolment at IITH and apply prior to completing their comprehensive examination.
